# EWTCS Staff Training Agenda

**Target Duration:** 45 minutes (strict)  
**Venue:** Hospital Computer Lab / Projector Room  
**Maximum Group Size:** 12 staff per session  
**Prerequisites:** System is live and accessible at training workstations

---

## Session Structure by Role

Run **separate** 45-minute sessions for each role. Do **not** mix Nurses with Supervisors.

| Session | Role | Duration |
|---------|------|----------|
| Session A | Nurses & Housekeeping | 45 min |
| Session B | Supervisors | 45 min |
| Session C | Admins | 45 min |

---

## Session A: Nurse & Housekeeping Training (45 min)

| # | Module | Format | Time |
|---|--------|--------|------|
| 1 | Welcome & System Purpose | Slides + Q&A | 5 min |
| 2 | The Bed Grid: Color Codes & Layout | Live Demo | 7 min |
| 3 | **Hands-On:** Login + update 3 bed stages | Practice | 10 min |
| 4 | Handling Delays (Red Tiles) & Delay Reasons | Live Demo | 5 min |
| 5 | **Hands-On:** Trigger a delay & select a reason | Practice | 5 min |
| 6 | Virtual Beds & Discharge | Live Demo | 5 min |
| 7 | What to do when something goes wrong (Undo / Call Supervisor) | Talk | 3 min |
| 8 | Quiz (paper or online) | Assessment | 5 min |

**Materials to hand out:** Nurse Quick Reference Card (`nurse-quick-reference.md`)

---

## Session B: Supervisor Training (45 min)

| # | Module | Format | Time |

**Materials to hand out:** Supervisor Quick Reference Card (`supervisor-quick-reference.md`)

## Session C: Admin Training (45 min)

| # | Module | Format | Time |
|---|--------|--------|------|

---

## Trainer Checklist (Before Session)

- [ ] All workstations can reach `http://localhost:3000` (or staging URL)
- [ ] Demo user accounts are seeded (admin1, supervisor1, nurse, nurse1)
- [ ] Printed QRC sheets are ready (1 per attendee)
- [ ] Quiz sheets printed (if paper) or quiz link shared (if digital)
- [ ] Projector / screen visible from all seats

## Trainer Checklist (After Session)

- [ ] Collect completed quiz sheets
- [ ] Distribute feedback forms and collect before attendees leave
- [ ] Record attendance (name, role, date, trainer)
- [ ] Enter quiz scores into HR training records
- [ ] Flag any attendees scoring <70% for a 15-minute follow-up session
