# Supervisor Quick Reference Card (QRC)

## ✅ AI Summary Review & Approval
1.  Navigate to the **AI Summary Section** at the bottom of the dashboard.
2.  Review any **"Pending"** drafts generated at midnight.
3.  Check key metrics: Total Patients, Avg TAT, Bottlenecks.
4.  **Edit** any narrative text if needed for accuracy.
5.  Click **"Approve"** to finalize the report for management.

---

## 🔧 Managing Temporary Beds
1.  Click **"Add Temporary Bed"** in the toolbar.
2.  Assign a temporary bed number (e.g., "Temp-B1").
3.  **To Remove**: Click the "X" on the bed tile when the surge ends.

---

## 🔍 Accessing the Audit Trail
1.  Locate the bed in question.
2.  Open the **"Edit History"** or **Auditor View**.
3.  Review timestamps and the specific user who performed each action.
4.  Log any non-compliance or systemic delays found.

---

## 📊 Interpreting Analytics
- **Red Zones**: Stages with the highest average TAT are your primary bottlenecks.
- **Hourly Heatmap**: Identifies peak admission and delay times.
- **CSV Export**: Use the **"Export Analytics"** button for deeper data analysis in Excel.

---

## 🚨 Override Procedure
1.  Select the bed that requires a correction.
2.  Open the **Override Modal**.
3.  Select the correct stage and provide a brief **Reason** for the override.
4.  Ensure the change is reflected on the Nurse Dashboard.
