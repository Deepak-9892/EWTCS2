# Nurse Quick Reference Card (QRC)

## 🎨 Color Code Legend
- 🟡 **Yellow**: Patient Admitted
- 🟠 **Orange**: Under Assessment
- 🔵 **Blue**: Tests Ordered
- 🟣 **Purple**: Awaiting Results
- 🟢 **Green**: Decision Made
- 🔴 **Red**: **DELAYED** (Exceeds time limit)
- ⚪ **Grey**: Discharged / Transferred

---

## ⚡ Quick Steps: Stage Update
1.  Locate the patient's **Bed Tile** on the dashboard.
2.  **Click** the current stage button (e.g., "Assessment").
3.  Select the **next stage** from the options.
4.  The tile will update instantly and the timer for the new stage will start.

---

## 🆘 How to Undo a Mistake
- Use the **"Undo"** button at the top of the dashboard if you accidentally clicked a wrong stage.
- If the undo window has passed, contact your **Supervisor** to perform an override.

---

## 📋 Patient Discharge Checklist
- [ ] Ensure all tests/decisions are documented in the system.
- [ ] Click the **"Discharge"** stage button.
- [ ] Confirm the discharge (this frees up the bed on the grid).

---

## 🛏️ Adding a Virtual Bed
1.  Click **"Add Virtual Bed"** in the top-right toolbar.
2.  Enter the patient's identifier (Hallway #1, Stretcher A, etc.).
3.  Click **"Create"**.
4.  Manage as a regular bed; discharge when the patient leaves.
