# Housekeeping Quick Reference Card (QRC)

## 🎨 Relevant Colour Codes
- ⚪ **Grey**: Bed is **empty and ready for cleaning**
- 🟡 **Yellow**: Bed occupied — Patient Admitted
- 🔵 **Hospital Blue (Cleaning stage)**: Bed **being cleaned** — you are responsible for this stage

---

## ⚡ Your Daily Workflow

### Step 1: Log In
1. Open the EWTCS dashboard on your workstation or tablet.
2. Enter your **username** and **password**.
3. You will land on the **Bed Grid** — your active cleaning assignments.

### Step 2: Mark a Bed as "Being Cleaned"
1. Find the bed tile that is **Grey** (patient discharged).
2. Click the bed tile.
3. Select the **Cleaning** stage — the tile will update to show it is being cleaned.
4. The timer starts automatically.

### Step 3: Mark Cleaning as Complete
1. Once cleaning is done, click the bed tile again.
2. Select **"Cleaning Complete"** or the equivalent next stage.
3. The bed is now available for the next patient admission.

---

## 🆘 Something Went Wrong?
- **Clicked the wrong stage?** Use the **Undo** button at the top of the screen immediately.
- **Undo window missed?** Contact your **Supervisor** — they can correct the stage.
- **System not loading?** Check your network connection, then try refreshing the page (F5).

---

## 📋 Shift Checklist
- [ ] All assigned discharged beds updated to "Cleaning" at start of shift.
- [ ] All cleaned beds marked "Complete" before shift ends.
- [ ] No Grey bed tiles left unacknowledged at shift handover.

---

## 🔒 Security Reminder
- Never share your login credentials.
- Always log out at the end of your shift (**Logout** button, top of screen).
- If you forget your password, contact your Supervisor or Administrator.
