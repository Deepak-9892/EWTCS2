# Supervisor Training Slides: EWTCS Oversight

## Module 1: Supervisor Oversight
### Monitoring Ward Health
- **Overview Dashboard**: View all beds and identify bottlenecks at a glance.
- **Delay Monitoring**: Focus on **Red** tiles to proactively manage delays.
- **Role**: Ensuring data accuracy and assisting staff with dashboard usage.

---

## Module 2: Managing Overrides & Delays
### Handling Exceptions
- **Supervisor Overrides**: Ability to adjust bed stages if a mistake was made.
- **Delay Documentation**: Reviewing the reasons recorded by Nurses for bottled-necked stages.
- **Intervention**: Identifying specific beds needing immediate attention or resources.

---

## Module 3: Daily AI Summaries
### Review and Approval Process
- **Drafts**: AI generates a summary of the day's operations at midnight.
- **Action Required**: Supervisors must **Review**, **Edit** (if necessary), and **Approve** the draft.
- **Outcome**: Once approved, the summary is published for Management review.

---

## Module 4: Analytics & Reporting
### Data-Driven Decisions
- **Turnaround Time (TAT)**: Track average time per stage and per patient.
- **Bottlenecks**: Visualize which stages cause the most delays (e.g., Lab delays vs. Radiology).
- **Trends**: Compare ward performance across different shifts or days.

---

## Module 5: Bed Management
### Temporary & Virtual Configurations
- **Add Temporary Bed**: Add extra physical beds during surge periods.
- **Remove Bed**: Clean up the dashboard when temporary resources are no longer needed.
- **Audit Trail**: Access the full history of status changes for any bed.

---

## Summary
- **Visibility**: Your oversight ensures the system remains a "Single Source of Truth".
- **Efficiency**: Use AI summaries to save hours on manual reporting.
- **Accountability**: Audit logs provide clear documentation of ward activity.
