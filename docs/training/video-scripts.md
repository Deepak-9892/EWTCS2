# Training Video Scripts

## Video 1: "Nurse Dashboard Basics" (3-4 mins)
**Audience**: Nurses and Front-desk staff.
**Goal**: Quick onboarding on bed status updates and time tracking.

### Scene 1: Introduction
- **Visual**: Close-up of the Nurse Dashboard with multiple bed tiles in different colors.
- **Narrative**: *"Welcome to the EWTCS Dashboard. This tool helps us track patient progress in real-time, ensuring no one is left behind in the emergency ward."*
- **[Subtitle]**: Intro to EWTCS: Visibility for the Emergency Ward.

### Scene 2: Updating a Bed Status
- **Visual**: Screen recording showing a cursor clicking on a "Yellow" tile and moving it to "Tests Ordered" (Blue).
- **Narrative**: *"When a patient's stage changes, simply click the current status and select the new one. It's that easy. One click updates the timer and syncs the dashboard for everyone."*
- **[Subtitle]**: One-click updates automatically track time.

### Scene 3: Handling Delays (The Red Tile)
- **Visual**: A tile turns red and shows a popup for selecting a delay reason.
- **Narrative**: *"If a patient exceeds the time limit, the tile turns red. Select a reason from the list. This helps your supervisor identify and resolve bottlenecks quickly."*
- **[Subtitle]**: Red tiles signal a delay. Select a reason to notify your supervisor.

### Scene 4: Virtual Beds
- **Visual**: Demonstrating the "Add Virtual Bed" button and creation of a hallway bed.
- **Narrative**: *"During peak times, use the 'Add Virtual Bed' button for hallway patients or stretchers. These beds are managed just like regular ones."*
- **[Subtitle]**: Manage overflow with Virtual Beds.

## Video 2: "Supervisor Oversight & Reporting" (5-7 mins)
**Audience**: Ward Supervisors.
**Goal**: Advanced features for ward health monitoring and AI report approval.

### Scene 1: Ward Overview
- **Visual**: The Supervisor Dashboard showing analytics and the full ward grid.
- **Narrative**: *"As a supervisor, you have bird's-eye visibility of the entire ward. Monitor delayed patients and proactively assign resources where they're needed most."*
- **[Subtitle]**: Supervisor oversight: Proactive delay management.

### Scene 2: Corrections and Overrides
- **Visual**: Opening the Override Modal and changing a stage with a reason.
- **Narrative**: *"Mistakes happen. If a stage was entered incorrectly, use the 'Override' function to fix the data. Every change is logged in the audit trail for transparency."*
- **[Subtitle]**: Data accuracy through controlled overrides.

### Scene 3: AI Daily Summaries
- **Visual**: Scrolling through the "Daily AI Summary" draft at the bottom of the page.
- **Narrative**: *"At midnight, the system generates a draft AI summary. Review the metrics, edit the narrative if necessary, and click 'Approve'. This becomes our official daily report."*
- **[Subtitle]**: Review and Approve AI-generated summaries.

### Scene 4: Using Analytics for Improvement
- **Visual**: Clicking into the "Analytics" tab and viewing the bottleneck heatmap.
- **Narrative**: *"Use the Analytics tab to identify persistent bottlenecks. Is the lab always slow on Monday nights? Our data will prove it, helping us make systemic improvements."*
- **[Subtitle]**: Drive improvements with data-driven analytics.

## Accessibility & Visual Standards
- **Subtitles**: All videos must have burnt-in English subtitles as indicated in the [Subtitle] tags.
- **Focus Highlights**: Use yellow circular highlights around the cursor during screen recordings.
- **Voiceover**: Clear, professional tone with pauses between major step-by-step instructions.
- **Visual Cues**: Use icons (Alerts, Bed Icons) to reinforce key concepts.
